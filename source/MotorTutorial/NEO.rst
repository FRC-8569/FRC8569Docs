NEO v1.1
++++++++
Basic Information:
    Type: Brushless motor

    Free Speed: 5676 RPM

    Motor Kv: 473 RPM/v

    Stall Torque: 2.6Nm
    
    Power@40A: 380W

    Wiring:12 AWG
    
    Controller: SparkMax

NEO Vortex
++++++++++
Basic Information:
    Type: Brushless motor

    Free Speed: 6784 RPM

    Motor Kv: 565 RPM/v

    Stall Torque: 3.6Nm

    Power@40A: 375W

    Wiring: 12AWG

    Controller: SparkMax, SparkFlex(recommend)
